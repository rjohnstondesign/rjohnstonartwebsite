03 — Process & Practice

The Studio as Laboratory

01

Observation

Every work begins with sustained looking — hours, sometimes days, before a mark is made. The hand must understand what the eye has seen.

02

Material Inquiry

Oil, encaustic, charcoal — each medium asks different questions. The work reveals itself through the resistance of the material.

03

Revision as Method

Overpainting, scraping, erasure. The visible history of a work is part of the work. Nothing is hidden.

04

Commission

Studio visits by arrangement. Bespoke works from three months. All enquiries handled personally.
